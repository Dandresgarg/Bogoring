Folklore

Folclore, ​​ folclor​ o folklore​ is the expressive body of culture shared by a particular group of people; encompasses the traditions common to that culture, subculture, or group

Free

A free alternative is "Bogotá ciudad del Folclor" This festival seeks to enhance the magic and color of popular festivities and the most representative folk expressions of the people.

Cheap

Another alternative is to go to Colombian folklore nights, where the average cost of a function is $25,000 to $50,000

Literature

Literature is defined as books and other written works, especially those considered to have creative or artistic merit or lasting value. Books written by Charles Dickens are an example of literature. Books written on a scientific subject are examples of scientific literature.

Free

Bogotá has 23 free libraries scattered throughout the city, with spacious and comfortable spaces, you can consult any information on the biblored page

Cheap

There are many options on the internet, facebook or in neighborhood cheap book stores

Painting

In art, the term painting describes both the act and the result of the action (the final work is called "a painting"). The support for paintings includes such surfaces as walls, paper, canvas, wood, glass, lacquer, pottery, leaf, copper and concrete, and the painting may incorporate multiple other materials, including sand, clay, paper, plaster, gold leaf, and even whole objects.

Free

Throughout the city of Bogota you can see street art without paying anything

Cheap

In Bogotá there are several museums, where the entrance is not expensive, there are museums where the entrance costs $1,000, $4,000, $12,000 or even the entrance is free, as in the Botero museum

Music

music, art concerned with combining vocal or instrumental sounds for beauty of form or emotional expression, usually according to cultural standards of rhythm, melody, and, in most Western music, harmony

Comedy

type of drama or other art form the chief object of which, according to modern notions, is to amuse. It is contrasted on the one hand with tragedy and on the other with farce, burlesque, and other forms of humorous amusement.

Free

There are many people who are made to tell funny stories on the street, in our university we have also had visits from some comedians, cuenteros, etc. In social networks we can see and support different types of comedians, FREE

Movies and TV

A film, also called a movie, motion picture or moving picture, is a work of visual art that simulates experiences and otherwise communicates ideas, stories, perceptions, feelings, beauty, or atmosphere through the use of moving images. These images are generally accompanied by sound, and more rarely, other sensory stimulations. The word "cinema", short for cinematography, is often used to refer to filmmaking and the film industry, and to the art form that is the result of it.

Free

For movies, it is easier to mention digital platforms, such as YouTube, or movies that, due to their validity, are shown for free on TV channels dedicated to showing movies, but we must not forget that sometimes there are events where they are shown on a big screen a movie

Cheap

More than anything, when a movie is just released, a good plan is usually to go to the cinema, in Bogotá there are many cinemas, from different companies, but it can be said that we all have at least one cinema nearby if we live in Bogotá,?

Recreation and sports

Recreational sports are those activities where the primary purpose of the activity is participation, with the related goals of improved physical fitness, fun, and social involvement often prominent. Recreational sports are usually perceived as being less stressful, both physically and mentally, on the participants and every Sunday and holidays there is a ciclovia.

Free

Bogotá currently has many parks for physical activity, from gyms to soccer fields, both freely and by reservation.

On the other hand, the district usually opens calls for courses in swimming, soccer, basketball, tennis, etc.

Cheap

There are many neighborhood gyms, in stratum 2 the monthly prices are on average $50,000, which in the end is not a lot of money, it is almost half the money we spend on transportation, and it is cheaper than a beer a day.
